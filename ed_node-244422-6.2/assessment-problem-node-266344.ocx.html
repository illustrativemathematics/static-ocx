To make fruit punch, Priya mixes 3 scoops of powder with 5 cups of water. Mai mixes 4 scoops of powder with 6 cups of water.

  1. Create a double number line or a table that shows different amounts of powder and water that taste the same as Priya’s mixture.
  2. Create a double number line or a table that shows different amounts of powder and water that taste the same as Mai’s mixture.
  3. How do their two mixtures compare in taste? Explain your reasoning.
