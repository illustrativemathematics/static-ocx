Constant Speed

Let’s use ratios to work with how fast things move.

I can choose and create diagrams to help me reason about constant speed. If I know that an object is moving at a constant speed, and I know two of these things: the distance it travels, the amount of time it takes, and its speed, I can find the other thing.
meters per second:

Meters per second is a unit for measuring speed. It tells how many meters an object goes in one second.

For example, a person walking 3 meters per second is going faster than another person walking 2 meters per second.
